Strategic Roadmap — Kitchen Applications Marketplace

Each phase builds on the last. Ship Phase 1 fast, then compound.

PHASE 1 Ship in 1-2 weeks

Close the Loop

Connect chefs to employers. Make every application land with impact.

  • Chef Profile Page — auto-generated from CV, attached to every outreach email
  • Employer Accounts — separate sign-up flow, restaurant claim/verify
  • Employer Inbox — see all applications, view chef profiles, mark interested/pass
  • Employer Verification Badge — verified restaurants get a trust badge

WHY FIRST: Without this, you have a tool. With this, you have a platform. Employers seeing polished profiles = the moment they start trusting you.

PHASE 2 Weeks 3-4

Revenue + Growth Engine

Turn users into revenue. Turn revenue into more users.

  • Subscription Tiers — Free (1 app) / Starter $19/mo (10) / Pro $39/mo (unlimited + interview prep)
  • Referral System — "Invite a chef, both get 1 free app." Viral loop for the tight-knit culinary world.
  • Placement Counter — "47 chefs placed" on homepage. Social proof that compounds daily.

WHY SECOND: Phase 1 gives you a platform. Phase 2 gives you a business. Subscriptions = predictable revenue. Referrals = organic growth. Counter = trust.

PHASE 3 Weeks 5-7

Engagement + Retention

Give people reasons to come back every week, not just when job hunting.

  • Employer Job Posting — post openings that appear on Job Board. Connects scraped + organic listings.
  • Seasonal Hiring Alerts — "Summer in Mediterranean starting — 12 new openings." Push notifications + email.
  • AI Interview Prep — mock interviews simulating a head chef. Pro tier upsell.

WHY THIRD: By now you have chefs + employers + revenue. This phase keeps them engaged and creates reasons to upgrade.

PHASE 4 Weeks 8-10

Trust + Moat

Build defensibility. Make it hard for competitors to replicate your data advantage.

  • Chef Score — composite reputation score (stars worked, experience, cuisine breadth, languages). Visible to employers.
  • Candidate Notes + Shortlists — employers add private notes, share with team, export lists. Mini-ATS.
  • Team Access — multiple employer users per restaurant. Head chef + HR can both review.

WHY LAST: Chef Score needs data (applications sent, responses received). Notes/shortlists need volume. Team access needs employer adoption. All require Phase 1-3 to exist first.
